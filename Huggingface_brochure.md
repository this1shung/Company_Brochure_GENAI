# Hugging Face Company Brochure

## Welcome to Hugging Face
Hugging Face is the AI community building the future. Our platform drives collaboration among the machine learning community, allowing for the sharing and development of innovative models, datasets, and applications. With a user-friendly interface and support for various modalities—text, image, video, audio, and even 3D—Hugging Face serves as the home for AI practitioners and enthusiasts alike.

---

## Our Offerings
### **Models**
Explore a vast library of **1M+ models**. From conversational agents to image processing, our community has developed cutting-edge models that cater to a wide range of applications.

### **Datasets**
Access **250k+ datasets** that are crucial for training machine learning models. These datasets are continually updated to ensure high quality and relevance.

### **Spaces**
Collaborate and share applications in **Spaces** with easy deployment options that allow you to run applications like image relighting or text-to-response generators in just a few clicks.

### **Enterprise Solutions**
Join over **50,000 organizations** that trust Hugging Face for their AI needs. Our enterprise offerings feature robust security, dedicated support, and advanced tools tailored for business applications.
